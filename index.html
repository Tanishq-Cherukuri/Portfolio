About Me

I'm a Senior SQL/BI Developer with 7 years of experience delivering end-to-end analytics across healthcare, finance, and enterprise domains — owning every layer from raw data ingestion to polished executive dashboards.

My expertise centers on dimensional modeling, cloud data platforms, and Power BI semantic layer design — the unglamorous backbone that makes dashboards trustworthy. I'm equally comfortable architecting a Snowflake star schema, tuning a complex T-SQL stored procedure, or designing DAX measures that CFOs will stake decisions on.

I also leverage Power Apps and Power Automate to replace clunky spreadsheet workflows with governed, automated pipelines — and use Power BI Copilot (GenAI) to accelerate DAX development while maintaining rigorous data governance.

Currently at St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, building the analytics infrastructure that helps researchers track treatment outcomes at scale.
